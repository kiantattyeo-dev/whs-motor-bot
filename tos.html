WHS Motor Bot is an internal social media automation tool operated by WHS Motor Banting for the purpose of posting motorcycle product photos to social media platforms including TikTok, Facebook, and Instagram.

This application is operated solely by WHS Motor Banting staff. It is not available to the general public. The application accesses TikTok on behalf of the business account only.

3. Content

All content posted through this application consists of motorcycle product photos and related sales information belonging to WHS Motor Banting. No user-generated content from third parties is collected or posted.

4. Data

The application stores TikTok API access tokens locally on the business's own computer. No personal data from TikTok users is collected or stored.
