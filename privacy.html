Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

1. Overview

WHS Motor Bot is an internal automation tool used by WHS Motor Banting to post motorcycle photos to social media. This policy explains how data is handled.

2. Data We Collect

The application collects and stores only the following:

  • TikTok API access tokens (stored locally on the business computer)
  • Product photos uploaded by WHS Motor Banting staff

We do not collect any personal data from TikTok users or third parties.

3. How Data Is Used

API tokens are used solely to authenticate with TikTok's API and post content on behalf of the WHS Motor Banting business account. Tokens are never shared with third parties.

4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on WHS Motor Banting's own computer. No data is uploaded to external servers by this application.

5. Third-Party Services

This application uses the TikTok Content Posting API. Use of TikTok is subject to TikTok's own Privacy Policy.
